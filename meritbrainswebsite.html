About Us

Merit Brains is a professional counselling service offering personalised support for individuals, couples, and families. I, Devashish Samanta, as your counsellor, will listen and guide you through life's challenges with empathy and expertise.

Your Professional Friend You Can Trust.

At Merit Brains, I conduct one-to-one counselling and help you through your mental journey. I have completed BBA and MBA, and have experience working in the corporate world. As I have an interest in human psychology, I gained expertise in this field by obtaining certifications like Certified Life Coach and NLP Practitioner. I have counselled children and adults and helped them achieve meaningful outcomes during sessions. You may not have control over external factors, but if you can change your internal attitude, everything around you will start to change.
